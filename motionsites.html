Core Structure

A centralized single-column architecture designed for immersive reading and high-focus content consumption. No distractions, just pure information flow.

  • Single-column center focus
  • Semantic sectioning
  • Distinct code block styling
  • Horizontal centering

Hierarchy

Strategic use of h2 through h4 elements to create a logical path for the eye to follow.

Readability

Optimized line-height (1.625) and max-width constraints to prevent eye fatigue.
